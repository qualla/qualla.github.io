features

Dozens of starter templates & guides make it easy
Qualla has many starter templates for the different types of activities and events you may need to coordinate.  They are pre-configured to make the activity a success, and you can customize them as you like.  Templates can come with Guides -- information to help you run activities like a pro.  You can also create your own templates to streamline things in the future, and share them with the rest of the Qualla community.  Create a Qualla mini website from a template. 
Qualla has many themes to pick from, as well as clip-art you can use. 
Or, if you'd like, you can upload your own image, choose a layout, and color, and you'll have a great-looking Qualla website! 
Make it Beautiful
Notifications
You can get instant, daily, and weekly notifications, as people work on your Qualla websites.  Coming soon: We're adding mobile apps as well, so soon you can get updates on your phones and tablets. 
Share via Facebook, Twitter, and beyond.  Qualla also features a simple invite-link you can share on your own. You can embed the short-link in e-mails, or use a QR code that can be scanned and converted into a link.  People you share with don't need to sign-in to use your Qualla website, if you don't want.
